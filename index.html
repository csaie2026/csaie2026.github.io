Welcome to attend CSAIE 2026!

The conference on Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Engineering (CSAIE'26) is an international event dedicated to advancing knowledge, collaboration, and innovation in the fields of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Engineering.

Organized by the SIMPA Laboratory of the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science at USTO-MB, Algeria, CSAIE'26 aims to bring together researchers, academics, PhD students, and industry professionals from around the world to present and discuss cutting-edge research, emerging technologies, and innovative applications.

The conference will provide an excellent opportunity for participants to exchange ideas, establish collaborations, and explore future research directions in rapidly evolving domains of computer science and artificial intelligence.

CSAIE’26, will be held on hybrid mode for attendees worldwide, taking place in Oran, Algeria, from 14 to 16 December 2026.

Authors are invited to submit original and high-quality research papers written in English via Submission section.

Publication

All submitted papers will undergo a rigorous peer-review process and will be evaluated by at least three independent reviewers based on originality, technical quality, relevance, and presentation.

Accepted papers will be submitted for possible inclusion in IEEE Xplore, subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements.

Only papers that fall strictly within IEEE’s technical scope are eligible for Xplore publication.

Submission

Authors are invited to submit original and high-quality research papers written in English.

Submitted papers must:

  • Present original work not previously published or simultaneously submitted elsewhere.
  • Follow the official IEEE conference paper format.
  • Be submitted in PDF format.
  • Not exceed 8 pages, including references, figures, and tables.
  • Clearly state objectives, methodology, results, and contributions.

Submission link: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/CSAIE2026/Submission/Index

Papers will be reviewed by the Scientific Committee and selected based on originality, technical quality, and relevance to the conference themes.

Submissions must adhere to the conference template:

  • Word version: https://template-selector.ieee.org/api/ieee-template-selector/template/550/download
  • LaTeX version: https://template-selector.ieee.org/api/ieee-template-selector/template/540/download

Authors are strongly encouraged to ensure their documents are formatted correctly according to the provided IEEE templates before uploading. Please be aware that submissions not following the guidelines may face delays or potential rejection during the administrative check phase.

About Oran and USTO-MB

Oran, known as "El Bahia" (The Radiant), is the second-largest city in Algeria and a vibrant center for culture, industry, and education. Situated on the Mediterranean coast, it offers a blend of historic charm and modern architectural development.

The University of Science and Technology of Oran Mohamed Boudiaf (USTO-MB) is a premier institution in Algeria. Since its founding, it has focused on scientific innovation and engineering excellence. It provides a world-class environment for research and international collaboration.

Visitors to Oran can explore historical sites like the Santa Cruz Fort, which overlooks the city, or walk along the Corniche for beautiful sea views. The city is also known for its unique musical heritage, particularly Rai music, and its bustling markets that offer a taste of authentic Algerian life. We look forward to welcoming you to this historic city for CSAIE'26.
